About Us

WHO WE ARE

Green Field Health Management (GFHML) Limited is a Health Maintenance Organization with a goal to achieve high quality and cost effective health care for its registered participants. Our strategy is to relieve the financial burden of healthcare from all categories of persons from the pool contribution of all participants. In addition to provision of curative services, we are also involved in promoting health and general wellness for our registered participants.

Our Board of Directors is composed of highly experienced and seasoned professionals in the field of Health Management and Administration, drawn from across the country. Our team of highly experienced and motivated staff, work round the clock to guarantee prompt and efficient provision of quality healthcare to enrollees.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality and comprehensive healthcare insurance services management to Nigerians utilizing high skilled manpower and best practices while according high priority to the welfare of our esteemed enrollees and healthcare providers.

Our Vision

To be among the best and fastest growing Health Maintenance Organization in the country through commitment to excellence and Industry best practices using the synergy of highly skilled people and technology.
